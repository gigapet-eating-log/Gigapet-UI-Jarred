Children are facing a health epidemic.

It is no longer a secret that many of the foods we have been feeding our children are unhealthy. Gigapet and parents need to come together and solve the adolescent health epidemic.

GigaPet redefines nutrition.

  1. Children pick from a variety of pet avatars to represent them on their healthy journey.
  2. Parents input the type and amount of food their child eats and Gigapet will track the nutrition.
  3. The healthier the child's diet, the stronger your pet will grow. Unhealthy foods make the pet's health diminish.
  4. The game aspect helps foster a connection between the parent, child, food and fun!

GigaPet is a healthy twist on the digital pet of the 90s.

"90s kids" are starting to have children of their own. Gigapet brings tech and nutrition together in a form that is full of nostalgia for parents and brings that same joy to your child that you had with your first Digital Pet.
